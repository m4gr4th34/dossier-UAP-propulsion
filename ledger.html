Conjecture 1 — conservation as a bubble-local symmetry. IF energy conservation is the shadow of our universe's (the bubble's) time-translation symmetry and the embedding meta-structure has no such symmetry, THEN total energy is undefined across the bubble boundary and energy need not be conserved there. Supporting crack: GR has no global energy conservation without a timelike Killing vector — our expanding universe has none (companion dossier). Cost: strains global energy conservation; requires an embedding meta-structure + a small-but-finite boundary coupling Γ (always-open floods, exactly-zero seals). Open hole: what sets Γ — and thus the measurable signature — is deferred to Part 2. Premise supposed, not asserted; gap is a contingent fact about reality, not verification work (so NOT OPEN-CAVEATED). Conjecture 2 — metric engineering (the warp branch). IF a craft can locally restructure the spacetime metric so its motion is decoupled from the surrounding medium, THEN the Track A anomaly is explained directly: no relative motion through the air, so no bow shock / fireball / sonic boom / thermal load. Predicted signature (answers the C09 bridge): ANCHOR = background-light lensing at the bubble boundary — not parallax, so the clean discriminator a balloon-at-wrong-range cannot fake; plus gravitational clock-rate shift, local tidal/field gradient, and the documented absence of LK signatures. Cost: negative energy density (Alcubierre-catastrophic magnitudes); violates GR energy conditions. Open vulnerability (the hinge): it OVER-PREDICTS — "why so subtle?" — an internal inconsistency left visible and unanswered. Gravitational-negation variant considered and dropped (no local GR knob; shielding experiments null). Adjacent grey-lit cited as speculative, NOT support (Alcubierre 1994; AAWSAP/DIRD). B2aTRACK B · PART 2 · PREDICTIVE REACH & BOUNDARY · 📌 PINNED
What else Conjecture 2 predicts. Predictive win (forward, unobserved): the warp premise is medium-independent for fluids, so trans-medium air↔water transition without a splash falls out for free (no relative motion → no momentum transfer → no splash) — a necessary consequence, not an add-on. Predicts FORWARD: zero confirmed transmedium cases in the AARO data, so a prediction seeking evidence, not an explanation of established data. Boundary (the finding): does NOT cleanly reach solid matter — either (a) displace it like a fluid → break/reform every lattice bond → bore/heat/damage, contradicting intact passage; or (b) decouple from matter entirely → contradicts the craft's observed coupling to air and light (why lens light and ride the air-frame but ignore rock?).
