IF energy conservation is the shadow of our universe's (the bubble's) time-translation symmetry and the embedding meta-structure has no such symmetry, THEN total energy is undefined across the bubble boundary and energy need not be conserved there. Cost: strains global energy conservation; requires an embedding meta-structure + a small-but-finite boundary coupling Γ (always-open floods, exactly-zero seals). Premise supposed, not asserted; gap is a contingent fact about reality, not verification work (so NOT OPEN-CAVEATED).
